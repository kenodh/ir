A Heartfelt Welcome to Mr. Thilak Waththuhewa, the New Principal of Royal College  2023-09-07    

It is with great pleasure that we extend a warm and sincere welcome to Mr. Thilak Waththuhewa, the newly appointed Principal of Royal College. With a remarkable career in education, and administration along with a wealth of experience, Mr. Waththuhewa has been fulfilling a crucial role full of dynamic leadership and fresh perspective in our esteemed institution since he assumed his duties on the 11th of August, 2023.

Dornhorst Memorial Prize 2023   2023-09-06    

The Dornhorst Memorial Prize, regarded as the most coveted prize of Royal College, is awarded to the Most Outstanding Royalist. Applications for the Dornhorst Memorial Prize 2023 are now open for all students of Grade 13 (2023 Advanced Level Batch). Applications must be handed over to the Main Office (weekdays only) and emailed to dornhorst@royalcollege.lk on or before 15th of September 2023 at 2.00pm. Applications handed over after the deadline will be rejected.
