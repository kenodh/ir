Moving with technology 

Royal College in keeping with the global technological trends has implemented an "online" system in order to track and monitor not only the academic progress of their children but also their discipline, attendance, sports and co- curricular activities. It also provides information on the other activities of the school, facilitating "discussion" the online system has made its mark in the globe village thus improve the quality of life at Royal.
